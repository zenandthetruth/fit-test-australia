Fit Test Australia Reviews

Fit Test Australia presents itself as a seasoned provider of fit testing services, claiming an operational history dating back to 2020 in Victoria. However, a closer examination of publicly available information, including ASIC records and their own website, reveals no established presence until 2024. This significant timeline discrepancy raises serious questions about the accuracy of their stated history.

Adding to these concerns is a pattern of aggressive review reporting on their Google My Business profile. While businesses have the right to report reviews that violate Google's guidelines, the sheer volume of reported negative reviews suggests a strategy aimed at suppressing critical feedback. This raises questions regarding their commitment to transparency and open communication with clients.

The lack of verifiable records prior to 2024, coupled with the apparent suppression of negative reviews, presents a complex picture. Were they operating under a different name, or without a registered business name and ABN, potentially misleading customers in the process? Or are their current claims about their history simply inaccurate?

To ensure transparency and provide a comprehensive record of customer experiences, we are creating an archive of all negative reviews received by Fit Test Australia. This archive will serve as a permanent record, capturing negative feedback, regardless of whether the original reviews remain visible on Google My Business.

We believe that open and honest feedback is essential for building trust and fostering accountability. By archiving these reviews, we aim to provide a valuable resource for individuals and organizations seeking fit testing services.
